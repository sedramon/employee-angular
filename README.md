Thank you for your interest in working for us. To assess your skills for the position you are applying for we have created a simple technical assignment. This assignment can be solved offline, in your own time. Once you have completed the assignment, please send us the public GitHub repo link of your solution, and email it to us. Based on the outcome of your solution you will enter the 2nd round of our hiring process.
